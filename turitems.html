    The following items are strictly prohibited in cabin baggage:

    ✗ Sprays used at home or during sport which are not flammable or toxic and do not cause any adverse side effects.;

    ✗ Toy guns or gun-shaped lighters;

    ✗ Wheelchairs and movement aids with non-leak batteries (except for electric-battery operated bicycles, Segweys, etc);

    ✗ Wheelchairs and movement aids with lithium batteries or batteries which may leak (except for electric-battery operated bicycles, Segweys, etc);

    ✗ Medical or clinical thermometers;

    ✗ Internal combustion or fuel cell engines;

    ✗ Sharp objects (penknives, knives, walking stick-style umbrellas etc.);

    ✗ Medical oxygen cylinders belonging to the passenger;

    ✗ Standardization devices;

    ✗ Safe bags, money bags, etc;

    ✗ Personal self-defense items such as pepper spray;

    ✗ Personal self-defense items such as electroshock devices;

    ✗ Camping stoves containing inflammable liquid fuel;

    ✗ Arms and ammunition;

    ✗ All cues (pool, billiard, etc.);

    The following are strictly prohibited in checked baggage:

    ✗ 1 packet of safety matches or 1 lighter (must not contain unabsorbed liquid fuel other than liquid gas);

    ✗ Foldable wheelchairs and movement aids with batteries or lithium-ion batteries (except for electric-battery operated bicycles, Segweys, etc);

    ✗ Spare lithium metal or lithium-ion batteries, or standard batteries;

    ✗ Thermometers or barometers containing mercury;

    ✗ Non-infectious samples in small quantities packed in flammable liquid;

    ✗ Safe bags, money bags, etc.;

    ✗ Personal self-defense items such as pepper spray;

    ✗ Personal self-defense items such as electroshock devices;
