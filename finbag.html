    Finnair's Bag Policy

    Carry-on Baggage

    Economy Superlight:

    • You can carry one piece of small bag (40 30 x 15 cm). Total weight of 8kg;

    Economy Classic/Flex:

    • You can one carry peace of Carry-on bag (55cm x 40cm x 23cm) and one piece of small bag (40cm x 30cm x 15cm). Total weight of 8kg;

    Premium Economy:

    • You can one carry peace of Carry-on bag (55cm x 40cm x 23cm) and one piece of small bag (40cm x 30cm x 15cm). Total weight of 8kg;

    Business:

    • You can one peace of Carry-on bag (55cm x 40cm x 23cm) and one piece of small bag (40cm x 30cm x 15cm). Total weight of 12kg;;

    *If your ticket type is Superlight, you can purchase a carry-on bag as a travel extra.

    Checked Baggage

    Economy Class and Premium Economy:

    • Maximum weight: 23kg;

    • External dimensions: up to 90cm x 75cm x 45cm;

    Business Class:

    • Maximum weight: 23kg on short-haul flights and 32kg on long-haul flights;

    • External dimensions: up to 90cm x 75cm x 45cm;

    *Finnair Plus Gold, Platinum and Platinum Lumo members can take one additional bag on flights operated and marketed by Finnair.
