airBaltic

Established in 1995, airBaltic is the national airline of Latvia, operating a modern Airbus A220 fleet. Renowned for its punctuality, the airline serves over 70 destinations with a focus on the Baltic region. As a leader in sustainability, airBaltic was the first airline to receive a five-star COVID-19 airline safety rating from Skytrax.
