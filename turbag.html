    Turkish Airlines Bag Policy

    1: Cabin Baggage

    Economy class:

    • You can carry one piece of cabin baggage (23 x 40 x 55 cm, MAX 8 KG) and one personal item (40 x 30 x 15 cm, MAX 4 KG);

    Business Class:

    • This allows you to carry two cabin bags (23 x 40 x 55 cm, MAX 8 KG, 16kg in total) and one personal item (40 x 30 x 15 cm, MAX 4 KG);

    *On domestic and international flights, economy and business class cabin baggage allowances for infant passengers are 1 piece/8 kg.

    2: Checked Baggage

    Economy class:

    • This allows you to carry one piece of baggage (158cm, MAX 23 KG);

    Business Class:

    • This allows you to carry one piece of baggage (158 cm, MAX 32 KG, 16kg in total);

    *Star Alliance Gold Card holders have the right to carry +1 extra baggage in the Piece System and +20kg in the Weight System in addition to their checked baggage rights on their tickets.
