    airBaltic's Bag Policy

    Cabin Baggage

    • You can carry one piece of cabinn bag (55 x 35 x 25 cm) and a free personal item such as a backpack, a handbag, a laptop and a camera(40 x 30 x 15 cm) in total weight of 8 kilograms unless you have upgraded your cabin baggage allowance up to 18 kg;

    Checked Baggage

    Economy:

    • 0 to 2 baggage items, depending on your chosen ticket fare (maximum weight per baggage: 23kg);

    Premium Economy:

    • 0 to 2 baggage items, depending on your chosen ticket fare (maximum weight per baggage: 23kg);

    Business:

    • 1 to 2 baggage items, depending on your chosen ticket fare (maximum weight per baggage: 32kg);

    La Première:

    • 3 baggage items (maximum weight per baggage: 32kg);

    *If you are a Flying Blue Silver, Gold or Platinum, or SkyTeam Elite or Elite Plus member, you are entitled to 1 additional checked bag at no extra charge. The permitted weight depends on your travel cabin.
