    Heston Airlines Bag Policy

    Carry-on Baggage

    • You can carry one piece of underseat bag (55 x 40 x 20 cm, MAX 5 KG) and a free personal item such as a backpack, a handbag, a laptop and a camera(30 x 40 x 10 cm).;

    • Any baggage that exceeds the carry-on baggage limit will be asked to transport as checked baggage, including any prohibited items like any fluid exceeding 100ml.

    Checked Baggage

    • You can carry a free baggage ( MAX 20KG, MAX 158cm);

    *Any checked baggage exceeding 32kg will not be accepted by the airline.

    Children And Infants

    ✓ Passengers travelling with infants receive additional checked-in baggage of 10kg and a personal item like a folding buggy;
